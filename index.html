What We Fix

Our Repair Services

We repair all major home appliances. Call us, describe the problem, and we'll schedule an in-home visit.

Washer & Dryer Repair

Won't drain, won't spin, not heating, or cycling on its own — we repair all washer and dryer makes and models including Whirlpool, GE, Samsung, and LG.

Oven & Range Repair

Gas or electric, wall ovens or freestanding ranges — igniters, heating elements, thermostats and control boards repaired correctly.

Refrigerator Repair

Not cooling, ice maker not working, unusual noises — we diagnose and repair refrigerators of all types and brands.

Dishwasher Repair

Not draining, leaving residue, won't complete a cycle — we service all dishwasher brands and restore full performance.

Diagnostic Service

Not sure what is wrong? We run a complete diagnostic, explain the issue in plain language, and give you a fair quote before any work begins.
